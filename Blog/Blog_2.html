Redesigning my speedrun blog


So, my previous attempt at this was awful. Really, really awful.


I'm a speedrunner, I've been following the speedrunning scene ever since I was 6-8 years old, but I haven't actually started until recently. It's very fun.

And so, the way I decided to show off my times on my site was by manually adding times and talking about the runs. Yeah, even for my obsolete PBs. It was very bad.


The reason for redesigning my site was super random, actually. I was searching the game All-Star Fruit Racing on Speedrun.com and came across a runner called "Jordanlol". They have a site dedicated to their runs. And the way they did it was 10x better than mine. Basically having a horizontal sidebar with their times displayed on them.

Check out the site! It's pretty neat. I decided to base my speedrun times like Jordan's, while not directly copying it, I will use a vertical sidebar, fixed to the left.


As this is a Boofny Tech project, of course, it has to have a name. I am really out of ideas, but I think that "Danin's Dashboard" would be cool.
