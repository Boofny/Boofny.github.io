Ryan's Rescue Squad


Yes.. another Ryan game...


Me and a Race with Ryan mod decided to run this game for the fun of it. Turns out, it's a really nice, relaxing platformer. Of course, it is a baby platformer, but still I was quite surprised that the developers actually put SOME effort into it.


But of course, with every Outright Games video game, there are countless glitches. You can clip through the floor, the enemy hit detection is shit, if you hit a ledge after jumping out of the water it sends you flying, and oh yeah! The bosses are recycled! Same way they attack, same way you attack them. Wow.


And as a speedgame? Not bad! It's really fun, and there is some "movement tech". Like using the clips to your advantage, and if you attack near a ledge it creates a boost.


An unfortunate thing tho, is that a different person is a mod for the game. What's sad is that mod is barely active, and even after reaeching out to them they still haven't responded. We really hope something will happen.


Anyways, the game is split into 4 worlds, each world having 5 levels, one minigame, and one boss. In every stage (except the minigames and the boss), there are collectibles, 3 R coins (probably Ryan coins), and 3-6 eggs. Chocolate Eggs? I have no clue. These collectibles serve no purpose than to unlock all costumes in the game.

Oh yeah! Even tho there are 4 characters, all of them are the same! Wow.


On Speedrun.com there are full game and individual level categories, both having any% and 100% categories.
